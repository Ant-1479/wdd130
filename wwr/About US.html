HISTORY

Splash whitewater rafting is a popular recreational activity around the world, with thousands of commercial rafting companies and countless enthusiasts. Advances in technology have led to the development of more durable and maneuverable rafts, as well as improved safety equipment. The sport continues to evolve, with new techniques, equipment, and destinations emerging all the time. The 1970s saw the emergence of commercial rafting companies, offering guided trips and instruction. The development of more advanced safety equipment, such as helmets and life jackets, made whitewater rafting safer and more accessible.
